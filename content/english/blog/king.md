Claire Sisco King is Associate Professor in the Department of Communication Studies and affiliated with the Department of Cinema and Media Arts. She is a scholar of media and visual culture, with a particular emphasis on the study of gender and sexuality. Her work has been published in numerous journals in the fields of communication and media studies.
