Laurel C. Schneider is Professor and Chair of Religious Studies and member of the Graduate Department of Religion at Vanderbilt University. Among other books and publications, she is the author of Beyond Monotheism: A Theology of Multiplicity (Routledge, 2007), co-author of Awake to the Moment: Introducing Constructive Theology (WJK, 2017) and co-author of Queer Soul, Queer Theology: Ethics and Redemption in Real Life (Routledge 2021).
